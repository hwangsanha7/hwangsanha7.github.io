The Dialectical Intellectual Alignment (DIA) Framework

My research explores how human-AI interaction can be designed as a transformative system that fosters high-order human cognition and the emergence of new concepts. The 'Dialectical Intellectual Alignment (DIA)' framework is my core theoretical and methodological proposal to answer this question.
